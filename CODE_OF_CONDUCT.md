# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, direct, and constructive. Focus feedback on the work and the behavior, not the person.

Examples of acceptable behavior:

- Clear bug reports with reproduction steps
- Good-faith technical disagreement
- Helpful review comments
- Respect for maintainers' time and project scope

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Publishing private exploits before maintainers can respond
- Spam, trolling, or repeated off-topic demands
- Sharing private credentials, webhooks, or server data

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through the Peak Studios Discord or repository maintainers.
